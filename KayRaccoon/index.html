Scenario:

You have been hired by a local amusement park to develop an online ticket system for customers. The amusement park has tickets for Senior, Adult, Child, and Student. You must show all of the ticket types and give customers a way to add them to the cart. Show the customers their cart and allow them to remove the last ticket they added.
